Welcome to Shobdosharothi.

Let your voice be your guide

Here you can review the supported voice commands and play with them. To get started, click the mic icon on the top right, and say 'navigate'. Out loud. Then you can say 'one', 'two'.. to relate to the menu item. Then 'navigate' again'. Etc etc.
